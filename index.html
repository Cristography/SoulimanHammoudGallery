About The Artist

Dedicated to the craft of pencil drawing

With a focus on graphite and charcoal, I create detailed drawings that capture the essence of my subjects. My work explores the interplay of light and shadow, bringing depth and emotion to each piece.

My artistic journey began with a simple pencil and sketchbook, evolving over years of practice to develop a style that emphasizes realism and attention to detail. Each drawing is a meditation on form and texture.
